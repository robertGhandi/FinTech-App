What are FundCoins?

FundCoins are like your digital rewards. You can earn them by doing things that help you build a stronger financial future. Here's how:

Invite Friends +20

Get your buddies on board. When they join Fundify using your invite, you earn FundCoins for every transaction they make.

Handle Your Money +20

Every time you do something smart with your money—like transferring, saving, or paying bills—Fundify thanks you with FundCoins.

Stay Active +20

Simply using Fundify regularly and checking in can add more FundCoins to your stash.
